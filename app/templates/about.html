Taking it to the next level

Empowering developers to build, tweak, and share compact code blocks.

Create, save, and share small pieces of code with others

By creating short pieces of code, you can easily test out new ideas and techniques without committing to a larger project. Saving these snippets allows you to refer back to them later and use them as a reference for future projects. Sharing these pieces of code with others can also be beneficial because it allows for feedback and collaboration, leading to even better results.

Keep track of the version and date as you modify code

This helps to ensure that you have a clear understanding of the changes that have been made to your code over time and can easily refer back to specific versions if needed. By keeping track of the version and date of your code, you can also more easily collaborate with other developers and share your work.

A source that is both extremely small and portable

Our app allows you to create, save, and share short pieces of code with others, all while keeping it compact with a maximum size of less than 3KB.
